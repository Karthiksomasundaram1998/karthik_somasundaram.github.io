Spark Plug Gap Determination

The project aimed to maintain the constant distance between the spark plug electrodes to uphold company standards and improve engine performance. The study utilized image processing techniques in MATLAB to determine the spark plug gap width, highlighting the impact of electrode distance on combustion, misfire, and fuel ignition. The findings underscored the importance of maintaining a standard spark plug gap to optimize engine functionality, with implications for fuel efficiency and overall performance.
